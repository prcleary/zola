    Paul Cleary
  • 10th Apr 2025
  • |2 min read
  • Just a quick post - I’m going to try to blog little and often in future
  • I’ve always known it was possible to create rates in DHIS 2, but it was more work than I expected when I came to do it; I’ve put my notes here as there were a couple of things I had to work out in the process; it’s not difficult though
